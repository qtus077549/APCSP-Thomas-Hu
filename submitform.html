To submit the course request, follow the instructions below:

  • Select 1 target course for each block
  • Select 1 supplement course for each block, but you do not need a supplement for your language course or your art course
  • Each person is to choose 4 humanities courses (2 for fall semester 2 for spring semester); 1 science course; 1 fine arts course; 1 world languages course; 1 mathematics course
  • If you select the same courses for your target and supplement courses, you will get a random course if your target course is not granted
  • If you select the same courses for two of your humanities course blocks, one of your courses will be randomly assigned
  • You will not be granted a course if you do not meet the prerequisites
  • Wrong names or personal informations would cause all of your courses to be randomly assigned
